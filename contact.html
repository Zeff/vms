Location

VMS is located in the heart of the Bluegrass. Minutes off the BlueGrass Parkway, VMS is conveniently located between Frankfort, Lawrenceburg, Lexington, Versailles and other nearby cities. Its 10 acre rural campus makes available small farm animals, organic gardens, a full size greenhouse, wildlife observations, and much more!
