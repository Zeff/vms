At VMS, our goal is to support the development of the whole child: academically, socially, physically and emotionally. This objective incorporates and prioritizes social and emotional factors along with academics and physical development.

Math, language, science and art are key areas of study, but learning to care for oneself, respecting others, resolving conflict and managing time effectively should be part of the whole educational package. Understanding this and knowing how to implement it lays a strong foundation, enriches a childâ€™s development and produces a responsible, healthy and creative citizen in their community.

We invite you to visit our programs to learn more and see how it works at the different age levels.
